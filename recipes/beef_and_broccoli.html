Beef and Broccoli

Description:

Beef and Brocoli is and easy, 1-pan, 30-minute meal that is loaded with fresh broccoli, tender nutition-packed beef, and the best stir fry sauce

Beef is delicious and protein-packed to give you the sustained strength you need to get through the back to school season. We also made sure

to add plenty of brocolli for a balanced meal.

Beef and Broccoli Ingredients:

  • 1 lb flank steak, very thinly siced into bite-sized strips
  • 2 Tbsp olive oil, (or vegatble oil), divided
  • 1lb broccoli, (cut into 6 cups of florets)
  • 2 tsp sesame seeds (optional garnish)

Stir Fry Sauce Ingredients:

  • 1 tsp fresh ginger, grated
  • 2 tsp garlic, grated
  • 1/2 cup hot water
  • 6 Tbsp low sodium soy sauce
  • 3 Tbsp packed light brown sugar
  • 1 1/2 Tbsp corn starch
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper
  • 2 Tbsp sesame oil

Instructions:

  1. Prep: Start cooking white rice first so it's ready when the stir fry is done. Cover and freeze steak 30 minutes for easier slicing.
  2. Combine all sir fry sauce ingredients in a bow, stir and dissolve sugar, set aside for later
  3. Place skillet on medium heat, add 1 TBSP oil. Add all broccoli, saute 4-5 mintues, partially cover with lid. Stir and toss several times until bright green.
  4. Increase to high heat and add 1 Tbsp oil. Add beef in and saute 2 miutes per side or until cooked through.
  5. Add sauce, reduce heat to medium/low and simmer for 3-4 inutes. Once thicken, add broccoli and stir to combine.
