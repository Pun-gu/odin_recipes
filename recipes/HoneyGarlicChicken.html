Honey Garlic Chicken

Descriptions:

This 5-ingredient, 15-minute honey garlic chicken with and addictively delicious sauce makes a perfect quick and easy weeknight dinner recipe!

Ingredients:

  • 2 teaspoons extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 1/2 lbs boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut into small cubes
  • Salt and black pepper
  • 3 Tbsp honey
  • 3 Tbsp low-sodium soy sauce
  • 3 Cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 tsp red pepper flakes

Instrctions:

  1. Heat olive oil in larger skillet over medium-high heat
  2. Lightly season the cubed chicken wit salt and pepper
  3. Add chicken to the skillet and brown on one side, about 3-4 minutes
  4. Meanwhile, makes the glaze. Whisk the honey, soy sauce, garlic and red pepper flakes, if using, in a small bowl until well combined
  5. Add sauce to the pan and toss to coat the chicken pieces. Cook until chicken is cooked through, 3-4 minutes
  6. Serve with steam rice and top with green onions and sessame seeds
