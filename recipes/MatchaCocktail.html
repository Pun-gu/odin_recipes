Matcha Cocktail

Description:

Muddled mint and agave gets shaken up with gin and matcha green tea for a bright and earhly cocktail that's great any time of day.

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup mint leaves
  • 3/4 ounce agave or honey
  • 3/4 ounce lime juice
  • 1 1/2 cups water
  • 2 1/2 ounces gin
  • 1 teaspoon matcha
  • Ice

Instructions:

  1. In a cocktail shaker, muddle the mint with agave and lime juice.
  2. Add the remaining ingredients and shake util well chilled and the matcha is dissolved.
  3. Strain through a fine-mesh strainer, dividing between 2 ice-filled rocks glasses, then serve.
